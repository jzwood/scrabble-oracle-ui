Scrabble Oracle

I'm often playing Scrabble and I idly wonder to myself what the highest scoring play is. This web app, Scrabble Oracle, answers this question for me (but you can use it if you want).

1. Upload Image

Click "Choose File" to upload or take a picture of the board (any perspective will do).

2. Crop Image

Drag the green markers onto the corners of the board (near corner triple word scores) so that only playable tiles are within the cropped region.

Output

3. Optical Character Recognition

You should now be able to see the entire board cropped tightly in grayscale. Click "Perform OCR" to extract letters from tile images.
